# How to Find Your Vercel Site URL

## Quick Method:

1. **Go to Vercel Dashboard:**
   https://vercel.com/dashboard

2. **Find your project:**
   - Look for "professors-keyboard" in your projects list
   - Click on it

3. **Get the URL:**
   - The production URL is shown at the top of the project page
   - It will be something like: `https://professors-keyboard.vercel.app`
   - Or: `https://professors-keyboard-xxxxx.vercel.app`

## Alternative: Check Recent Deployments

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click on "professors-keyboard" project
3. Click "Deployments" tab
4. Click on the most recent deployment
5. The URL will be shown there

## Common Vercel URL Patterns:

- `https://professors-keyboard.vercel.app`
- `https://professors-keyboard-[your-username].vercel.app`
- `https://professors-keyboard-[random-id].vercel.app`

Your deployment should be live in 1-2 minutes after the push!
